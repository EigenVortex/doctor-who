Welcome to the Vortex

The "EigenVortex" — a portmanteau indexing the analytical power of Eigenvector centrality and the allure of the Time Vortex — is a project that quantifies social interactions within Doctor Who stories and transforms that data into an interactive visualization. The latest version of this visualization is below.


Behind the Scenes

  • Corpus Assembly

    A story of interest from the Whoniverse is identified and necessary files are pre-processed for data extraction.

  • Data Extraction

    With the power of natural language processing, the frequency of directed interactions is extracted from each story.

  • Finishing Touches

    A directed graph is built from those interactions and fashioned into an interactive visualization.
