Overview

Below is an interactive social network constructed from the book Doctor Who and the Ark in Space by Ian Marter. Within the graph, the search tools in the window on the left and the scaling tools at the bottom can help you find your way around quickly. Mousing over a node will reveal the character it represents. To move the graph, click and drag it. To view a character up close, click their node — this will display their full name, modularity, in-degree, out-degree, and eigenvector centrality. It also displays a clickable list of their connections to other characters.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter a problem with the graph display, there will be a link below the visualization to view the network in a new tab. If you cannot view the graph at all, please take a look at the EigenVortex GitHub page, where you can download the visualization files and host the interactive tool locally. If you have display issues with multiple graphs, please consider sending an email to eigenvortex@gmail.com to alert us to the problem.
